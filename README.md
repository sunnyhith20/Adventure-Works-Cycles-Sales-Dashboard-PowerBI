Adventure Works Cycles Sales Dashboard
An interactive multi-page Power BI dashboard for Adventure Works Cycles — a fictional manufacturer and retailer of bicycles, components, and accessories.
This project provides a complete business intelligence view covering sales performance, product profitability, order details, customer demographics, and financial metrics.
📊 Dashboard Pages & Key Features

Home / Overview: High-level KPIs (Sales: 29.36M | Profits: 12.08M | Total Product Cost: 17.28M)
Sales Analysis: Model by sales, sub-category performance, country-wise sales map, gender breakdown, and monthly tracking
Sales & Product Analysis: Top 10 products by sales & profit, yearly sales vs. cost trends, detailed product table with color, unit price, and margins
Order Details: Monthly order quantity heatmap, model by quantity, total cost by sub-category/month, and recent customer orders
Customer Details: Individual customer profiles with personal info, purchase history, shipment dates, reviews, and suggestions
Report View: Consolidated finance insights

🎯 Business Insights Delivered

Identified top-performing products (Mountain and Road bikes dominate sales)
Analyzed profit margins across products and years
Visualized customer demographics (age groups, gender)
Tracked seasonal/monthly sales patterns
Detailed cost vs. sales analysis for better pricing and inventory decisions

🛠️ Tech Stack

Power BI Desktop – Visualization & Reporting
Power Query – ETL, Data Cleaning & Transformation
DAX – Calculated measures for profits, margins, and KPIs
Relational Data Model (Fact & Dimension tables – Star Schema)

Dataset
Based on the classic AdventureWorks sample database (bike sales data covering 2010–2013).
How to Use

Download the .pbix file
Open with Power BI Desktop
Interact with slicers (Year, Quarter, Month, Country, etc.)
